tha safer approach
in most emergencies, we need to think about our own safety and tha safety of others. rushing in to situation may endanger the rescuer as well as causing unnecessary mistakes that may lead to danger. to prevent this happening, you need to learn the SAFER approach:
S - step back, never rush in, step back physically and amotionally
A - assess the situation and make sure there are no dangers to yourself before approaching
