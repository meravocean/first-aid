cardio pulmonary resuscitation
check for signs of circulation for not more than 10 seconds. if there are no signs of a pulse, commence CPR
feel for the lowesst ribs where they meet the breast bone with your finger tips. with your opppsite hand, place the heel of the hand where your index finger tip is this is where you will carryout the compressions.
give 30 compressions using straight arms, depressing into the chest for 1 1/2 to 2 inches at the rate of 100 compressions per minute.
