home cpr for a child
cardio pulmonary resuscitation
once you have completed 30 compressions, give a further 2 rescue breaths and continue this cycle of 15 compressions to 2 breaths until professional emergency help arrives.
if casualty moves or takes a spontaneous breath, check for pulse and then place in the recovery position.
if there are two trained people on the scene that can carryout this procedure, then take it in turns so that you do not become exhausted.
